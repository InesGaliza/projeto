Hi! My name is Inês Galiza

I’m a design student, born in Porto in 2001.
Currently, I study Communication Design at the Faculty of Fine Arts of Porto, Portugal.

Since a young age I have been fascinated with arts and music. I am a music lover and addicted to listening to music all day, but it is also an inspiration to all my work. I really enjoy travelling, especially camping in the summer to meet new people and have new experiences.

In my work, I always try to bring something different to represent the image in my mind. I like to work between both the print and digital worlds, as I cannot settle with a single type of design or style. In addition, my interests are building visual identities, websites, editorial publications and I especially have an interest for film and photography.

ILLUSTRATION

GIRLS OFF SKATE

2020

"Girls off skate" is a project that aims to high-light women in the skate world. This little pink booklet takes on some of the prejudice that female athletes suffer daily and, by swapping genders, creates irony filled scenes that serve to acentuate common stereotypes and call them out.

IDENTITY

DYSTOPIA

2020

"Dystopia" is a collection composed of three of what i consider to be the best books of the 20th century. Although all of them were written over fifty years ago, their messages still stand to this date. The thought behind the concept for this project relies precisely in this fundamental aspect.

FILM & PHOTOGRAPHY

DEVELOOP

2021

A short-film created in colaboration with Francisca Rebelo, Rita Melo and Inês Leal. "Sara waits impatient while she watches the time pass by. Meanwhile, arrives an unknown boy and with a misterious package, whom occupies a near seat.
